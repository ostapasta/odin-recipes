Fruit Loops

My personal favorite cereal.

Fruit Loops are a classic cereal choice. Who doesn't like Fruit Loops? Here I'll show you how to make the most delicious bowl you've ever had.

    Ingredients
  • Whole Milk
  • Fruit Loops
  • Bananas
  • Blueberries
    Steps
  1. Pour the whole milk into the bowl. Make sure it's whole milk. Anything else is trash.
  2. Put the fruit loops into the milk.
  3. Cut up some banana and put it into the bowl.
  4. Add blueberries.
  5. Eat it up.
