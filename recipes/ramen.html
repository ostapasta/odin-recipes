Ramen

My personal favorite meal.

Ramen is my favorite dish of all time. It's so simple, yet elegant, and can be made in so many different ways with many ingredients. My personal favorite is very simple.

    Ingredients
  • Ramen noodles
  • Seasoning packet
  • American cheese
    Steps
  1. Put the ramen noodles into a bowl of water. Make sure the bowl is microwave safe.
  2. Pour the seasoning packet over the bowl of ramen.
  3. Microwave ramen until desired texture is achieved.
  4. Add American cheese slice.
  5. Eat it up.
